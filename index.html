Help About Us
"MEAL MARVEL," is a platform that allows users to discover a variety of tasty meals. The user can click on the random meal generated on the homepage to get details about it, including ingredients and instructions. Additionally, users can search for specific meal categories, and the website dynamically updates the content based on the selected category. The navigation bar includes options for Home, Categories (with dropdown functionality), and links for Help and About Us. The design features a clean and user-friendly interface, making it easy for visitors to explore and find meal inspiration.
